# Privacy Policy for Stellar Cargo

**Effective Date:** March 6, 2026

Thank you for using **Stellar Cargo** ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how Stellar Cargo handles information when you use our Progressive Web App (PWA) available at [https://stellar-cargo.pwabucket.com](https://stellar-cargo.pwabucket.com) and listed in the PWA Bucket store. By using Stellar Cargo, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Who We Are

Stellar Cargo is an open-source, non-custodial Stellar network wallet developed and maintained by the PWA Bucket team. The application runs entirely in your browser as a Progressive Web App with no backend server of our own.

---

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

Stellar Cargo is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do **not** collect, transmit, process, or store any of the following:

- Your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information
- Your Stellar account secret keys or seed phrases
- Your wallet balances, transaction history, or account activity
- Your IP address or device identifiers
- Usage analytics or behavioural tracking data
- Cookies or similar tracking technologies

All wallet data — including account keys and settings — remains exclusively on your device in your browser's local storage. We have no access to this data at any time.

---

## 3. Non-Custodial Architecture

Stellar Cargo is a **non-custodial** wallet. This means:

- Your private keys are generated and stored locally on your device only.
- No private keys or secret keys are ever sent to any server operated by us.
- You are solely responsible for safeguarding your private keys and seed phrases. If you lose access to your keys, we cannot recover them for you.
- Transactions are signed locally in your browser and then broadcast directly to the Stellar public network.

---

## 4. Stellar Network Interactions

When you use Stellar Cargo to send or receive transactions, your transaction data is submitted to the **Stellar Public Network**, which is a decentralised, publicly accessible blockchain. Transaction data submitted to the Stellar network is inherently public and visible to anyone querying the blockchain. We do not control the Stellar network and are not responsible for the public nature of on-chain data.

Stellar Cargo communicates with publicly available Stellar Horizon API servers to fetch account information and submit transactions. These requests may carry your Stellar public key as a query parameter. We do not log or store these requests on any server we control.

---

## 5. Google Drive Integration

Stellar Cargo offers an optional **Google Drive backup** feature. If you choose to enable this feature:

- You will be prompted to sign in with your Google account and grant Stellar Cargo permission to access your Google Drive.
- Stellar Cargo uses this permission **solely** to read and write your encrypted backup file in your Google Drive.
- Your backup data is **encrypted on your device** before being uploaded. We do not have access to your encryption key or the decrypted contents of your backup.
- We do **not** access, read, share, or store any other files in your Google Drive.
- The Google Drive integration is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). Please review it to understand how Google handles your data.
- You may revoke Stellar Cargo's access to your Google Drive at any time through your [Google Account permissions settings](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

Stellar Cargo does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only third-party integration is the optional Google Drive backup described above.

Your browser or device may independently collect usage data according to its own privacy policy. We have no control over and no access to any such data.

---

## 7. Data Security

Because all sensitive data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers, the security of your wallet relies primarily on the security of your device and browser. We recommend:

- Keeping your operating system and browser up to date.
- Using a strong device lock screen or password.
- Never sharing your private keys or seed phrases with anyone.
- Making regular encrypted backups using the Google Drive backup feature or another secure method.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

Stellar Cargo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has used this app in a way that raises privacy concerns, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
